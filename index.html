CHICKEN MALAI BOTI:

    Chicken malai tikka / malai boti is a Pakistani / Indian recipe made using chicken cubes
    marinated in yoghurt, cream, spices then grilled.

INGREDIENTS:

  1. Boneless chicken (cubes)
  2. Cardomum powder
  3. Green chilli (chopped)
  4. Ginger (chopped)
  5. Garlic (chopped)
  6. Yogurt
  7. Vinegar
  8. Lemon juice
  9. Oil
  10. Fresh cream
  11. Salt
  12. While pepper powder

METHOD:

  • Take a above and mix all ingredients with all boneless chicken pieces.
    Marinate it for 1 hour minimum or you can marinate chicken over night it will become more tendy and juicy

  • coal it on coal on medium heat apply oil on half done

  • Garnish with letuce,tomatos,cucumber,serve hot
